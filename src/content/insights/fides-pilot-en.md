On December 19, 2025, Fides (Vietnam) Fund Management JS Company ("FIDES Vietnam") and Aureus Sigma Capital JS Company ("ASC") signed a memorandum of understanding at the FIDES office in HCMC. The MoU establishes a framework for cooperation on investment mandate execution, product development, and research and analytics within the applicable regulatory framework.

FIDES Vietnam is an asset management firm headquartered in Vietnam with core businesses in fund management, portfolio management, and securities investment advisory. It currently manages portfolios for institutional and individual clients and public funds investing in Vietnam’s capital markets.

ASC is a Vietnam-based systematic investment systems firm focused on research, portfolio design, execution-aware models, and risk-control infrastructure for disciplined capital allocation. ASC develops and reviews systematic frameworks that combine market data, company information, trading constraints, and predefined risk limits to reduce short-term emotion and bias in investment decisions.

Under the cooperation framework, ASC provides research and portfolio-design support, while FIDES Vietnam retains sole discretion for investment decisions, portfolio implementation, risk management, and regulatory compliance.
