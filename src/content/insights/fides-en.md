FIDES, a licensed investment manager with Korean roots operating in Vietnam, and Aureus Sigma Capital, a proprietary systematic trading and investment firm focused on Vietnam’s capital markets, have signed a strategic memorandum of understanding.

Vietnam’s stock market has grown rapidly, yet many investment and trading decisions are still made from short-term opinions. Both firms see an opportunity to study disciplined, rules-based systems while keeping investor protection and compliance boundaries clear.

Aureus Sigma Capital designs, tests, and maintains research, data, execution, portfolio-construction and risk-control systems. The research process blends market data, company information, trading constraints and predefined guardrails.

FIDES is responsible for any regulated products, onboarding, suitability, execution, reporting and client-facing obligations under its licence.

Under the partnership, ASC may provide rulebooks, research updates, diagnostics and risk oversight. FIDES determines how any regulated strategies are deployed within its licensed vehicles. ASC does not offer products directly to the public and does not handle client funds through this website.

Over time the firms plan to study systematic approaches in Vietnam while holding to one principle: transparent rules and risk management first.
